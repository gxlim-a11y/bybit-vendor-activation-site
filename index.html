❌ CPM / CPC / CPI / Signup
Based on historical data these buy types do not perform to Bybit's ROI expectations and are not recommended. Campaigns under these models are unlikely to be approved.
Bybit's core principle: We evaluate ROI on every vendor investment. If eFTDs are not trading, or trading below payout levels, that traffic is classified as less valid or invalid. We are exclusively targeting high-quality users who deposit and trade.
✅ eFTT-Core Buy

What is eFTT-Core?
Deposit ≥ $100 AND first trade within 30 days. Bybit's primary revenue event — only active traders generate fees.

Conversion Flow:

👁️ Impressions → 👆 Clicks → 📲 Install → ✍️ Signup → 🪪 KYC → 💰 eFTD → 📈 eFTT-Core
Trial Budget $5,000
Trial Period 4–5 weeks
Post-trial Pause if targets not met

Why preferred: Aligned with company goals — only trading users generate revenue. Higher budget & faster onboarding for vendors who agree.

⚠️ eFTD Buy

What is eFTD?
Deposit ≥ $100 within 14 days of sign-up. Does not directly impact OKR or revenue.

Conversion Flow:

👁️ Impressions → 👆 Clicks → 📲 Install → ✍️ Signup → 🪪 KYC → 💰 eFTD
If deposit < $100: 👁️ → 👆 → 📲 → ✍️ → 🪪 → 💵 FTD (not billable)
Trial Budget $3,000
Trial Period 3 weeks
No trade = Deposited but no action after
Post-trial No trade/fees → closed. Vendor notified to pause.

⚠️ Higher Risk: Not Bybit's core objective. Team monitors closely to avoid loss. Non-trading traffic = invalid.

Key Points for Confirmation
All points must be acknowledged before onboarding
🔐
PID / AFPRT Requirement — Macro String
Before launching any campaigns, a macro string must be appended to the AFPRT/PID (VTA/CTA). Bybit will provide standardized template links for you to apply the macro directly.

The c=parameter is a mandatory Bybit parameter. If you do not agree to this or modify it, Bybit reserves the right to withhold payment.

Example: trendingcoinevergreen_17301743002311598
⚠️ Any AFPRT/PID running links not aligned with our required format will result in eFTDs not being billable.
🛡️
Fraud Policy
Our internal risk team flags fraudulent users based on abnormal behaviours at the eFTD and eFTT level, including but not limited to:
  • Use of externally purchased KYC services
  • Multiple fake or emulated devices abusing reward mechanisms
  • High eFTD volume with no subsequent trading activity
Such users will be classified as invalid and excluded from billing. A consolidated fraud report is shared at the end of each month and applies to all vendors.
🚀
Campaign Briefing & Launch Flow
Campaign assets (budget, creatives, tracking links) will be shared via email. Do not launch any campaign without prior written confirmation from Bybit.

  • All tracking links must be adjusted to match our internal attribution requirements.
  • Do not run any unmodified links — links without Bybit parameters will not be paid.
  • Do not remove existing parameters (additional parameters may be added if required).
Non-compliance may result in eFTDs/eFTTs being deemed invalid and non-billable.
🌍
Restricted Markets
Advertising is not permitted in the following countries:
Singapore Malaysia Thailand Philippines Canada Australia New Zealand United States Uzbekistan Indonesia Hong Kong Japan
💳
Billing Validation
Bybit validates and pays only for eFTDs/eFTTs reflected in the AppsFlyer raw report that meet both conditions:
  • IS Primary Attribution = TRUE
  • Retargeting = NO
📊
Attribution Rules
Signup: First-touch attribution
eFTD: Last-click attribution
eFTT-Core: Last-click attribution

Once a user signs up under your account or campaign ID, the signup remains attributed to you. Other vendors cannot hijack or redirect this data to claim the eFTD.
Background Information
  • We are currently using AppsFlyer only as our Mobile Measurement Partner (MMP).
  • All partnerships operate on a CPA model.
  • FTD — triggered when a user makes their first deposit.
  • eFTD — triggered when a user deposits ≥ USD 100 within 14 days of their FTD.
  • eFTT-Core — triggered when a user deposits ≥ USD 100 and completes their first trade within 30 days of eFTD.
  • Payout pricing will be shared after your IO submission. All vendors start with the same initial payout. Once your UA users demonstrate higher trading activity or user value, we will submit an approval to increase your payout.
